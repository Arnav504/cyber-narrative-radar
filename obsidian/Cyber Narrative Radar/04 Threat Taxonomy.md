# Threat Taxonomy

## Categories

- Data breach
- Ransomware
- Phishing / social engineering
- Zero-day / critical vulnerability
- Supply chain compromise
- Deepfake / disinformation cyber influence

## Signals

### Data breach
- exposed data
- leak
- stolen records
- unauthorized access
- compromised account data

### Ransomware
- ransom
- encrypted systems
- extortion
- systems offline
- recovery disruption

### Phishing / social engineering
- impersonation
- spoofing
- credential theft
- fake login
- malicious email campaign

### Zero-day / critical vulnerability
- exploit
- unpatched
- actively exploited
- critical CVE
- remote code execution

### Supply chain compromise
- vendor compromise
- third party
- dependency
- software compromise
- update mechanism abuse

### Deepfake / disinformation cyber influence
- fake executive audio
- manipulated video
- impersonation campaign
- coordinated influence
- synthetic media deception
