# Portfolio Story

## One-line pitch

I built a cybersecurity narrative intelligence MVP that transforms fragmented public discourse into explainable early-warning alerts.

## What it demonstrates

- Cybersecurity reasoning
- Applied NLP
- Data engineering
- Dashboard design
- Product thinking
- Explainable analytics

## Interview framing

This project is inspired by the broader public concept of narrative-intelligence platforms, but adapted to cybersecurity. The focus is on explainable analyst workflows rather than opaque automation.

## What to emphasize

- The scope is intentionally narrow and MVP-friendly
- The system uses public and synthetic data responsibly
- The scoring is interpretable and evidence-based
- The project shows end-to-end implementation
