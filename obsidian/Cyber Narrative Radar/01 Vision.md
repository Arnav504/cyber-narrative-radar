# Vision

## Problem

Analysts face too much fragmented public information across news, blogs, forums, and social channels.

## Hypothesis

Cyber incidents and cyber-enabled influence campaigns leave early traces in public discourse.

## Product concept

Cyber Narrative Radar monitors public chatter, detects unusual shifts, classifies likely threat narratives, and surfaces explainable alerts.

## User

- Cyber analyst
- Risk analyst
- Policy researcher
- Security-minded executive
