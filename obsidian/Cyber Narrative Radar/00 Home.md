# Cyber Narrative Radar

## Purpose

A portfolio project that detects emerging cybersecurity narratives from public online chatter and turns them into explainable alerts for analysts.

## Why this project matters

- Bridges cybersecurity, AI, OSINT, and policy
- Demonstrates applied analytics and product thinking
- Shows how noisy discourse can become a structured decision-support signal

## Core question

Can public chatter reveal emerging cyber-risk narratives affecting organizations or sectors before the issue is fully visible elsewhere?
